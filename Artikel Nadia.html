INTERNET OF THINGS (IOT)

Internet of things is a concept where an object is embedded with technologies such as sensors and software in order to communicate, control, connect, and exchange data through other devices as long as they are connected to the internet.IoT is closely related to the term machine-to-machine or M2M. All devices that have M2M communication capabilities are often called smart devices. These smart devices are expected to help human work in completing various affairs or tasks at hand.

IoT is closely related to the term machine-to-machine or M2M. All devices that have M2M communication capabilities are often called smart devices. These smart devices are expected to help human work in completing various affairs or tasks at hand. The development of technology in Indonesia is growing day by day. New breakthroughs are starting to emerge, one of which is the Internet of Things or what can be abbreviated as IoT. Actually, IoT itself has been developed for a long time, but recently its name has become increasingly popular.

The benefits of the Internet of Things (IoT) are :

1. Data can be integrated directly and can be accessed anywhere easily.

2. Practical and more efficient in one time.

3. More efficient in carrying out tasks and working with the internet of things.

4. For developers, the internet of things can help understand consumer behaviour.

5. Access to real-time information.

6. Cost reduction.

7. Improved business efficiency and productivity.

8. Improved monitoring with sensors.
